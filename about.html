Porsha has found a passion in paying it forward. Working in education for over 6 years and recognizing the gaps in when it comes to representation, she decided to fill the gap. Creating L.I.T. Kids, LLC. was an way to give kids the opportunity for connection, to be inspired while learning. Not remembering many books of color being accessible to her during adolescence, she did not want her son to experience the same. A native of Austin, TX and a proud graduate of Clark Atlanta University, she is striving to be a trailblazer in youth work through education, literacy development, and her soon-to-debut children's book, Cocoa Delight and Her Chocolate Chip Tears, written to teach children compassion and self-awareness through embrace your unique beauty.
